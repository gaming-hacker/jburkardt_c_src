Ourfa oresca andwa evensa earsya agowa ourwa athersfa oughtbra orthfa onwa 
istha ontinentca awa ewna ationna, onceivedca inwa Ibertyla, andwa edicatedda 
ota etha opositionpra attha allwa enma arewa eatedcra equalwa.

Owna ewa arewa engagedwa inwa awa eatgra ivilca arwa, estingta etherwha attha 
ationna, orwa anywa ationna, osa onceivedca andwa osa edicatedda, anca ongla 
endurewa. 

Ewa arewa etma onwa awa eatgra attleba-ieldfa ofwa attha arwa. 

Ewa aveha omeca ota edicateda awa ortionpa ofwa attha ieldfa, aswa awa inalfa estingra 
acepla orfa osetha owha ereha avega eirtha ivesla attha attha ationna ightma 
ivela. 

Itwa iswa altogetherwa ittingfa andwa operpra attha ewa ouldsha oda istha.

Utba, inwa awa argerla ensesa, ewa anca otna edicateda - ewa anca otna 
onsecrateca - ewa anca otna allowha - istha oundgra. 

Etha avebra enma, ivingla andwa eadda, owha uggledstra ereha, aveha 
onsecratedca itwa, arfa abovewa ourwa oorpa owerpa ota addwa orwa etractda. 

Etha orldwa illwa ittlela otena, orna ongla ememberra atwha ewa aysa ereha, 
utba itwa anca everna orgetfa atwha eytha idda ereha. 

Itwa iswa orfa uswa etha ivingla, atherra, ota eba edicatedda ereha ota etha 
unfinishedwa orkwa ichwha eytha owha oughtfa ereha aveha ustha arfa osa 
oblyna advancedwa. 

Itwa iswa atherra orfa uswa ota eba ereha edicatedda ota etha eatgra askta 
emainingra eforeba uswa - attha omfra esetha onoredha eadda ewa aketa 
increasedwa evotionda ota attha auseca orfa ichwha eytha avega etha astla 
ullfa easurema ofwa evotionda - attha ewa ereha ighlyha esolvera attha 
esetha eadda allsha otna aveha iedda inwa ainva - attha istha ationna, 
underwa Odga, allsha aveha awa ewna irthba ofwa eedomfra - andwa attha 
overnmentga ofwa etha eoplepa, bya etha eoplepa, orfa etha eoplepa, allsha
