SPHERE_INTEGRALS
Integrals on the Surface of the Unit Sphere in 3D

SPHERE_INTEGRALS, a C library which returns the exact value of the integral of any monomial over the surface of the unit sphere in 3D.

The surface of the unit sphere in 3D is defined by 

        x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 1

The integrands are all of the form 

        f(x,y,z) = x^a y^b z^c
where the exponents are nonnegative integers. If any exponent is an odd integer, the integral will be zero. Thus, the "interesting" results occur when all exponents are even.
