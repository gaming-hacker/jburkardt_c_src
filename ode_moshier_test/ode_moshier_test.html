ode_moshier_test

ode_moshier_test, a C program which calls ode_moshier() to solve systems of ordinary differential equations. Two main methods are implemented, the Adams-Bashforth-Moulton integrator, and the Runge-Kutta integrator, by Steven Moshier.

Related Data and Programs:

ode_moshier, a C library which implements the Adams-Bashforth-Moulton and Runge-Kutta (RK) methods of solving systems of ordinary differential equations (ODE's), by Steven Moshier.
